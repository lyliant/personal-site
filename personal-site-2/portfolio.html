About

Lylian Thao, a Hmong-American artist from the Bay Area, uses her art to address meaningful topics and inspire change. Drawing from her family and cultural background, her work focuses on the artistic responsibilities of Hmong women and aims to educate and empower them.

Her art, which incorporates symbolism, encourages self-reflection and promotes themes of self-love and self-care. Through her creations, she honors her heritage, inspires reflection, and advocates for positive change, emphasizing the value and importance of every individual's life.

Artist Statement

Lylian Thao, a Hmong-American artist from the Bay Area, uses her art to address meaningful topics and inspire change. Drawing from her family and cultural background, her work focuses on the artistic responsibilities of Hmong women and aims to educate and empower them.

Guided by my affinity with symbolism, I invite viewers to delve into the depths of introspection, exploring themes of self-love and self-care that resonate deeply within my work. I skillfully intertwines storytelling elements through my characters and scenes, offering glimpses into rich narratives that evoke empathy and understanding. Utilizing the dynamic capabilities of digital illustrations and 3D models, I bring my experiences and ideals to life with vivid detail and immersive depth, fostering empathy, understanding, and positive change through my art.
